PELS (Pris- og Effektstyrt Energilagringssystem) is for homes where EV charging, heating, hot water, or ventilation can push an hour over a tariff step or breaker limit. It watches whole-home power from Homey Energy, Tibber Pulse, AMS/HAN/P1 meters, or Flow data, and limits lower-priority loads before the hard cap is crossed. When available power returns, PELS resumes them in priority order.

For price-aware setups, PELS can factor in Norwegian spot prices, grid tariffs, strømstøtte/Norgespris, Homey Energy prices, or external Flow prices. Smart tasks schedule suitable hours so an EV, room, or water heater can be ready by a chosen time while priorities decide what waits first.
