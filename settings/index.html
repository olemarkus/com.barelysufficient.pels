    Managed Limit Price Explain ▸
    • Managed

      PELS observes this device's state. Required before Limit or Price can take effect.

    • Limit

      PELS will actively turn the device on and off to safely fill the allocated budget. When disabled, PELS will only turn the device on when it has a smart task, and will not turn it off if it was switched on externally.

    • Price

      PELS shifts the device's temperature target with electricity prices — boosting on cheap hours and easing back on expensive ones.
